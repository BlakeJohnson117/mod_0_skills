City Park

Acres (Float)
Amenities (Array)
Alcohol Permitted (Boolean)
Parking Spaces (Integer)

Add_Amenities (This will add more amenities to the park. It will have an effect on the amenities attribute)
Update_Alcohol_Policy (This will update if alcohol is allowed within the park.  This method will have an effect of the Alcohol Permitted attribute)
Remove_Parking_Spaces (This will remove parking spaces in case the city wishes to cut down on fillable parking spaces.  This will have a direct effect on the Parking Spaces attribute)
